## 2. Triggering Events

Two simultaneous geopolitical shocks create incremental illicit actors and transaction volume beyond steady-state:

### Event 1: Venezuela — Regime Collapse & Sanctions Flux
- **Jan 3, 2026:** Maduro captured by US forces; $700M assets seized (including Cap Cana, DR mansion)
- **80%** of PDVSA oil revenue now flows through USDT stablecoins (Tether/Tron)
- **Nov 2025:** TPS termination for ~300K Venezuelans in US
- **Dec 2025:** SDN list expanded at unprecedented pace — dozens of new designations
- **Feb 2025:** Tren de Aragua designated as FTO; Treasury targeted TdA financial infrastructure
- **Ongoing:** Operation Southern Spear — largest US Caribbean military presence since Cuban Missile Crisis

### Event 2: Mexico — Dual Cartel Decapitation
- **Aug 2025:** Ismael "El Mayo" Zambada pled guilty; Sinaloa homicides up 400%
- **Feb 22, 2026 (4 days ago):** "El Mencho" Oseguera Cervantes killed; CJNG in succession crisis
- **Jan 2025:** 8 cartels designated as Foreign Terrorist Organizations
- **Jun 2025:** FinCEN designated 3 Mexican banks under FEND Off Fentanyl Act (first-ever use)
- **2024–2025:** 6+ DEA prosecutions of Dominican fentanyl distribution networks
- **2025:** DR gold/jewelry exports surged +42%; OECD flagged FTZ illicit gold flows

**Key distinction:** The baseline estimation (Feb 24, 2026) captured steady-state illicit activity. This document estimates the **marginal increase** caused specifically by these events — new actors entering the system, existing actors intensifying, and new channels opening.

## 9. Sanity Checks

### Check 1: Incremental vs. Baseline Proportion
- Incremental at Scotiabank (mid): 166 actors = **13.3%** of baseline 1,250
- Within 10–40% target range: **PASS**
- Detectable incremental: 130 = **28.9%** of baseline 450 (higher because incremental skews toward detectable categories)
- Within range: **PASS**

### Check 2: Combined Base Rate vs. Industry Benchmarks
- Combined base rate (all illicit): 0.28% — still within 0.004%–0.3% benchmark
- Combined detectable rate: 0.116% (1 in ~860) — within 1:1,000 to 1:25,000
- **PASS** — at high end of range, consistent with high-risk jurisdiction under geopolitical stress

### Check 3: STR Volume Proportionality
- Incremental STRs (mid): ~53/year = **16.3%** increase over baseline 325
- Compare to incremental detectable actors: +28.9%
- STR increase is less than actor increase because not all actors generate STR-worthy activity immediately
- **PASS** — proportionate

### Check 4: Category Plausibility
- Largest incremental category: Money mules (600 mid in DR, 41 at Scotia) — consistent with I28 (mule demand scales with proceeds volume) and the 5x seizure increase
- Smallest: Chinese MLN (60 mid in DR, 4 at Scotia) — appropriate given DR is secondary to US/Mexico corridor
- PEP capital flight (250 mid in DR) — plausible given Maduro's inner circle numbered in the hundreds and DR is a confirmed destination
- **PASS** — no category dominates implausibly

### Check 5: Investigator Workload (Revised)
- Revised Year 2: ~38 STRs/month + ~1,000 alerts/month

### High-Impact Uncertainties

1. **El Mencho killing recency (4 days ago):** The CJNG fragmentation effects are just beginning. Actual displacement could be larger or smaller than estimated depending on succession dynamics. This estimate may need revision in 30–60 days.

2. **VZ2 TPS/Migration category:** Most speculative. Range is 3x between low and high. Actual irregular migration to DR depends on US deportation policy, DR enforcement capacity, and alternative destinations.

3. **Crypto off-ramp scale:** The $70B+ USDT volume is enormous, but how much reaches DR banking is unknown. Our estimate of 100 actors (mid) could be an order of magnitude too low or too high.

4. **Baseline double-counting risk:** Some "incremental" actors may have been implicitly included in the baseline (e.g., balloon effect was already underway in Feb 2024). We mitigate this by explicitly scoping incremental to the marginal effect of recent events.

5. **Temporal distribution:** These shocks don't create all incremental actors on day one. The full impact may take 6–18 months to materialize. Near-term impact may be lower; long-term may be higher.
