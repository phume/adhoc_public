## 9. The Deduplication — "~30%" and What It Means

### What is deduplication?

The 7 categories are **not mutually exclusive**. The same person can appear in multiple categories:

- A **corrupt customs official** (PEPs) who **facilitates cocaine transit** (DTOs) = double-counted
- A **money mule** (mules) who also **distributes fentanyl** (fentanyl) = double-counted
- A **lawyer** (ML professionals) who works for a **DTO** (DTO facilitators) = double-counted
- A **street dealer** who also acts as a **low-level mule** = double-counted
- A **PEP** who runs a **nightclub front** for DTO proceeds = double-counted

### Why 30%? — Quantified overlap matrix

**[A] ALL overlap estimates in this matrix are assumptions. No empirical data exists on cross-category overlap rates for DR criminal actors.**

Using the mid-estimate (62,600 total before dedup):

| Overlap pair | People double-counted | % of 62,600 | Tag | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| DTO facilitators ↔ PEPs | [A] ~800 | 1.3% | [A] | Most PEPs who facilitate OC are also DTO facilitators |
| DTO facilitators ↔ ML professionals | [A] ~400 | 0.6% | [A] | ~400 of 500 ML pros also serve DTOs |
| Fentanyl network ↔ mules | [A] ~1,500 | 2.4% | [A] | ~30% of fentanyl street dealers double as mules |
| DTO operatives ↔ fentanyl network | [A] ~500 | 0.8% | [A] | Some DR-based DTO operatives also in fentanyl supply |
| Street dealers ↔ mules | [A] ~2,000 | 3.2% | [A] | Some dealers lend accounts for money movement |
| Street dealers ↔ fentanyl network | [A] ~3,000 | 4.8% | [A] | ~20% of full-time dealers sell fentanyl network product |
| PEPs ↔ ML professionals | [A] ~100 | 0.2% | [A] | Lawyer-politicians |
| Human trafficking ↔ DTOs | [A] ~300 | 0.5% | [A] | Shared logistics infrastructure |
| Other minor overlaps | [A] ~500 | 0.8% | [A] | Various |
| **Total pairwise double-counted** | **[A] ~9,100** | **~14.5%** | | |

**Why 30% and not 15%?**
- [A] The matrix counts only **pairwise** overlaps. Some individuals appear in **3+ categories** → adds ~5-8%.
- [A] Within-category overlaps (e.g., US-side mule who is also DR-side mule) → adds ~5-7%.
- [A] **Final: ~15% pairwise + ~8% triple + ~7% within ≈ ~30%**

### The math

| | Before dedup | × [A] 0.70 | After dedup |
|---|---|---|---|
| Low | 21,150 | × 0.70 | ~14,805 → **~15,000** |
| Mid | 62,600 | × 0.70 | ~43,820 → **~44,000** |
| High | 125,900 | × 0.70 | ~88,130 → **~88,000** |

### Sensitivity — what if 30% is wrong?

| Dedup factor | Mid-estimate after dedup | Δ from base |
|---|---|---|
| [A] 20% (less overlap) | 62,600 × 0.80 = ~50,000 | +14% |
| **[A] 30% (base case)** | **62,600 × 0.70 = ~44,000** | — |
| [A] 40% (more overlap) | 62,600 × 0.60 = ~37,500 | -15% |

The range is ~37,500-50,000 for the mid. Doesn't change downstream conclusions materially.

### What it is NOT

- **Not** a precision discount
- **Not** a banking penetration adjustment (that comes later)
- Purely an **overlap correction**
