## 9. The Deduplication — "~30%" and What It Means

### What is it?

Categories are **not mutually exclusive**. Same person in 2+ categories = double-counted. Examples: corrupt official who facilitates DTO transit; lawyer who launders for DTOs; street dealer who also mules.

### Why 30%? — overlap matrix

**[A] ALL overlap estimates are assumptions.**

| Overlap pair | Double-counted | % of 62,600 | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|
| DTO facilitators ↔ PEPs | [A] ~800 | 1.3% | Most PEPs who facilitate OC also counted as DTO facilitators |
| DTO facilitators ↔ ML pros | [A] ~400 | 0.6% | ~400 of 500 ML pros serve DTOs |
| Fentanyl ↔ mules | [A] ~1,500 | 2.4% | ~30% of fentanyl street dealers double as mules |
| DTO ↔ fentanyl | [A] ~500 | 0.8% | Some DR-based DTO operatives also in fentanyl supply |
| Street dealers ↔ mules | [A] ~2,000 | 3.2% | Some dealers lend accounts |
| Street dealers ↔ fentanyl | [A] ~3,000 | 4.8% | ~20% of full-time dealers sell fentanyl product |
| PEPs ↔ ML pros | [A] ~100 | 0.2% | Lawyer-politicians |
| Trafficking ↔ DTOs | [A] ~300 | 0.5% | Shared logistics |
| Other | [A] ~500 | 0.8% | Various |
| **Pairwise total** | **[A] ~9,100** | **~14.5%** | |

[A] + ~8% triple-counting + ~7% within-category ≈ **~30% total**
